These sites are interesting in a 70/30 kind of way. They expose you to interesting links from the world wide (i.e. humungous) web, but the links that rise to the top must be liked and not disliked by the majority, which then forces you to ask the question: “how close to the majority am I”. Each site has a slightly different flavour of majority and it is always changing, typically from uber technical early adopters into a more mainstream majority.
